About Me

Drafted in 2007 out of high school by the Florida Marlins (now Miami Marlins), I went on to play 4 seasons of professional baseball in their organization. During that time, I was able to refine my skills with some of the best coaching in the business. Although my dream of making it to the Major Leagues did not happen, the knowledge I received and learned while playing was invaluable. My goal now is to pass along my baseball knowledge to the younger generation, in the hopes of helping current baseball players become future Major Leaguers.

Lessons

The lessons I conduct start with a throwing program, followed by a mixture of hitting, fielding, and baserunning. If you would like to focus on one area over another, please let me know.

Hitting

Becoming a better hitter starts with tee work. Getting the proper swing mechanics is crucial to developing into a proper hitter. In addition to tee work, I also do soft toss drills in order to develop rhythm and timing.

Fielding

As an infielder, I know how important footwork is to becoming solid defensively. I stress the importance of a quick first step and soft hands. I also work with outfielders upon request.

Baserunning

In order to become an all around athlete, you must be able to run the basepaths correctly. From basestealing, to being fundamentally sound running the basepaths, I teach students the keys to running bases. I also like to do agility drills, such as ladder work, to increase the athlete's quickness.
